Where to place your humidifier

Do not use humidifier outdoors

Do not place humidifier in an area accessible to children

Do not place humidifier near heat sources such as stoves, radiators, and heaters

Do not place housing under water flow or immerse in liquids

Place humidifier in an area where a 120V AC electrical outlet is accessible

Keep the cord out of heavy traffic areas

Never put the cord under rugs or near heat registers

Place humidifier on a firm, flat, level surface. A waterproof mat or pad is recommended for use under the humidifier

Do not place it on a rug, carpet, or on a finished floor that may be water damaged

Do not allow the Moisture Outlet to directly face the wall

Do not place humidifier under overhanging surfaces

Do not place anything over moisture outlet when the unit is running

Do not use humidifier in a closed room

Do not use humidifier in an area where humidity level is in excess of 50%

Do not place humidifier less than 4 inches (10 cm) away from the wall

Do not excessively humidity a room
